Using your dashboard

This is your control room. Changes save to your browser instantly and preview on the blog — to go live, you Export.

  1. Posts / Pages tabs (top-left) — switch between blog posts and standalone pages like About or Contact.
  2. New post — fill in the details, write the body with the toolbar (bold, headings, lists, links), then Save.
  3. Edit / delete — the pencil and trash icons on each row.
  4. Featured star — sets the big hero post on the home page (only one at a time).
  5. Draft vs Published — drafts stay hidden from the public blog.
  6. Search & filter chips — find posts fast by title, category, or status.

To publish to your live site: click Export → Download posts-data.js, replace that file in your blog folder, then re-deploy.
